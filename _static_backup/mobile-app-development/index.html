Frequently Asked Questions

Can you help if I only have an idea and no designs?

Yes. We often start from scratch with clients — we’ll help with discovery, UX flows, and full UI design before development begins.

Do you build for both iOS and Android?

Yes. We can build cross-platform apps for both stores, or focus on a single platform first depending on your strategy and budget.

Can you integrate with my existing backend or ERP/CRM?

In most cases, yes. We’ll review your existing systems and design clean, secure integrations via APIs or middleware.

Will I own the code?

Yes. You retain full ownership of the code, assets, and IP. We can also hand over documentation and support onboarding your internal team.

Do you offer ongoing maintenance?

We do. We can continue as your long-term technical partner for updates, fixes, and new feature releases.
